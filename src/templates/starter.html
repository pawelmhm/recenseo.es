Need a review of your draft?

Recenseo.es is simple app which allows you to share your articles, drafts, books etc with others. The idea is simple, you can publish your text and get a fast review from other users.

You publish your draft

Simply upload its title and attach a pdf,doc or other file. You can chose to remain anonymous if you'd like to.v

Someone reads it

They can read your draft and write a brief feedback for you.

You get the feedback

And can improve your work before sending it to a journal or publisher.
